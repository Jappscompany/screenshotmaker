Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the app OCRBook ("the App") handles user data.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All images, templates, and generated content remain on the user's device.

Files and Photos Access

The App may request access to your photo library or Files app solely for the purpose of importing images and documents selected by the user. No data is uploaded to external servers without user consent.

AI Translation (Ollama)

When using the AI-powered text generation or translation feature, the App communicates with a user-configured Ollama instance. The App does not log or store the content processed by AI.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the App Store support page.
